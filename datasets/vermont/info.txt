Description: <p>This dataset contains votes for 15 different races for various public offices held in Vermont in 2014.  This data was collected and donated by <a href="https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Jeremy_Hansen2">Jeremy A Hansen</a>.  There are 3 to 6 candidates and 532 to 1960 voters in these data files.  Not all races were competitive so not every race is reported for every district.</p>
